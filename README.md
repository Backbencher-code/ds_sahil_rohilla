# Bitcoin Market Sentiment vs Trader Behavior Analysis

## Overview
This repository presents an analysis of how Bitcoin market sentiment influences trader behavior
and performance on the Hyperliquid trading platform. The study combines the Bitcoin Fear & Greed
Index with historical trade-level data to examine changes in profitability, risk exposure, and
trading activity across different market sentiment regimes.

The analysis period spans **December 2024 to April 2025** and focuses primarily on Bitcoin (BTC)
trades to ensure direct alignment with the sentiment indicator.

## Notes

- Bitcoin market sentiment is treated as a macro-level risk indicator rather than a directional
  trading signal.
- Leverage effects are inferred indirectly through position sizing due to the absence of explicit
  leverage data.
- Findings are observational and do not imply causality.
